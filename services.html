Tatto Removal with Picco Laser

This laser can be applied to almost any part of the body such as the face, chest, arms, and legs. The Pico laser is relatively new on the market and it can effectively remove different tattoo colors thanks to the two wavelengths and powerful Pico pulse. It works in one trillionth of a second. Low Level Laser Therapy

Low level laser therapy (LLLT) has been increasingly used worldwide for the treatment of Androgenetic alopecia and other forms of hair loss. Low level laser therapy (LLLT) is one of the very few non surgical modality of hair loss treatment approved by the US FDA (2007). The lasers used in LLLT are typically small (often handheld) units, and unlike surgical and aesthetic lasers, do not heat the targeted tissue. Instead, LLLT emits low levels of light that are absorbed by your mitochondria (the energy-producing organelles in many of your cells), which then can increase cellular energy production and ultimately help heal surrounding tissue. This process is comparable to plant photosynthesis, during which sunlight is absorbed by plants and converted to energy for the plants to grow.

Hydrafacial And Oxygen Facial

HydraFacials and OxyGeneo facials are forms of microdermabrasion. This is the process of improving the skin cells by mechanically powered abrasion, often accompanied by the application of topical treatments. OxyGeneo facials differ in many ways from HydraFacials allowing for improved results.

The machine used for HydraFacials uses rotating liquid with flushing motion to exfoliate and infuse the skin. There are four steps to a HydraFacials and each one requires a different tip to be placed on the end of the machine. The first step uses water to exfoliate and remove dead skin cells. The second step uses salicylic acid and glycolic acid to treat debris embedded in pores to help loosen it for removal. The third step uses water in a vortex cycle to remove the dirt and debris that can lead to acne. The final step requires serum for the liquid and infuses the skin with a myriad of benefits.
